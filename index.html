About Me

I'm Callum (sometimes known as Bagel), a game developer based in Scotland currently studying Games Development at the University of the West of Scotland (UWS).

I'm passionate about creating imaginative and thoughtful games that spark creativity and curiosity. I love experimenting with ideas, blending technical design with storytelling to build worlds that feel alive and inspiring.
